Common Questions

Quick answers to help you start your event journey

How soon should I contact you?

We recommend contacting us at least 3-6 months before your desired event date for optimal planning. However, we can accommodate urgent requests for events within shorter timelines.

What happens after I submit the form?

Within 24 hours, our event consultant will contact you for an initial discussion. We'll then schedule a detailed consultation to understand your needs and prepare a custom proposal.

Do you handle international events?

Yes! While based in Kosovo, we have extensive experience managing events across Europe and can coordinate international logistics, multilingual support, and cross-cultural considerations.

What is your typical response time?

We guarantee an initial response within 24 hours for all inquiries. For urgent matters, you can call us directly at +383 44 123 456 for immediate assistance.
